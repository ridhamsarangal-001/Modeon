# Content Guide — Modeon

## Brand Voice
Quiet luxury. Minimal, refined, understated. Every word is intentional — no exclamation points, no urgency tactics, no filler. Sentences are short and confident. The brand speaks the way premium products are made: with restraint.

- "Where less becomes more."

## Product Naming Style
Editorial, article-led naming. Material and technical specifications belong in the description, not the title.

Examples:
- "The Essential Coat"
- "The Signature Shirt"
- "The Tailored Trouser"
- "The Classic Knit"
- "The Refined Blazer"
